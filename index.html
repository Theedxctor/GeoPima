Frequently Asked Questions

What services do you provide?

▼

We offer comprehensive land surveying services, including title deed processing, land mapping, boundary surveys, property subdivision, and property searches. Our team is committed to accuracy and precision in all our services.

Why is a land survey important?

▼

A land survey is essential for establishing clear property boundaries, which helps prevent disputes and ensures accurate documentation. It’s also necessary for title deed processing, construction planning, and determining property value.

What documents do I need for a land survey?

▼

Generally, you will need your title deed, land registration details, and any previous survey records. Our team will guide you on additional documents required based on the specific survey service you need.

How accurate are your surveys?

▼

We use advanced technology and industry-standard practices to ensure high accuracy in our surveys. Our team is professionally trained and certified to deliver reliable results, minimizing the risk of errors or discrepancies.

How can I schedule a land survey?

▼

You can schedule a survey by contacting us via our website, phone, or email. We will arrange an initial consultation to understand your requirements and provide a project estimate and timeline.
